Learning from large scale textual corpora and its applications in Information Retrieval and Natural Language Processing are my favorite research interests. It's an emerging hot topic in the intersection of IR, NLP and ML. In particular, I am interested in deep learning models that go beyond the typical search engines to teach them how to understand text documents and user's information needs in order to search smarter. I am also interested in understanding how current models scale to very large collections, such as WWW. Learning conceptual representation of textual documents is a follow up research work. Currently, I specialize in the area of sequence to sequence models. I am interested in the following topics:

  • Deep Learning:
    • sequence to sequence models
    • encoder--decoder models
    • neural machine translation
    • semantic embedding
    • multi-task transfer learning
  • Information Retrieval:
    • web document ranking
    • query reformulation
    • understanding user's query intent
    • clustering/classification
  • Natural Language Engineering:
    • dialog systems
    • machine translation/transliteration
    • semantic relatedness
    • topic modeling
